History

Nit Calicut Health Center,CALICUT was stated in the year 1987 under the guidance of Dr. A. K. RASTOGI who had a dream and vision to make quality healthcare affordable and accessible to all. This desire and the mission to meet the growing healthcare needs of the region culminated in the formation of health center, which revolutionised the very concept of healthcare in Kerala It is a multidisciplinary, 10-bed, tertiary care hospital.
