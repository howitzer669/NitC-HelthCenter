Welcome to Nit Calicut Health Center




Our leading private physicians offer patients coming to The Physicians' Clinic expertise across the following medical specialties:

Acute and general medicine

  • Diagnosis and care for private patients experiencing new and perhaps severe symptoms.
  • Medical checkups.
  • Treatment for hypertension and assessment of cardiovascular risk.
  • 365 day per year acute care hospital admission service providing emergency assessment.
  • Pre-operative checks and fitness for surgery tests.
  • Post-operative care, including transition from high dependency care.

Cardiology

  • Diagnosis of coronary artery disease.
  • Treatment to lower cardiovascular risk.
  • Assessment of cardiac function.
  • Treatment for heart muscle disorders.
  • Assessment and treatment of heart failure.
  • Diagnosis and treatment for congenital heart problems.
  • Detection of problems with heart rate and heart beat.
    •

Dermatology

  • Common skin conditions.
  • Skin cancer; detection and treatment, including Mohs micrographic surgery.
  • Hyperhydrosis treatment.
  • Assessment and therapy for excessive blushing.
  • Male genital dermatology.
  • Assessment and treatment of HIV-related skin disorders.
    •

Endocrinology and diabetes

  • Diagnosis and treatment of type 1 diabetes and type 2 diabetes.
  • Assessment and treatment of thyroid disorders.
  • Diagnosis and management of pituitary disorders.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of polycystic ovary syndrome.
    •

Gastroenterology and hepatology

  • Investigations for unexplained symptoms such as diarrhoea, abdominal pain.
  • Blood tests and scans (MRI, CT and ultrasound).
  • Endoscopy investigations including gastroscopy, colonoscopy and ERCP.
  • Liver function tests, including FibroTest.
  • Interventional HPB radiology.
    •
  • Liver biopsy.

Haematology

  • Diagnosis and treatment of blood clotting disorders.
  • Haemophilia.
  • Von Willebrand's disease.
  • Thrombosis treatment and prevention.
    •

Infectious diseases

  • Diagnosis and treatment of infection caused by bacteria, viruses, fungi and parasites.
  • Management of chronic viral hepatitis.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of tuberculosis.
  • Prevention of blood-borne infections.
    •

Respiratory medicine

  • Assessment and diagnosis of breathing problems.
  • Diagnosis of sarcoidosis.
  • Diagnosis, staging and treatment of lung cancer.
  • Assessement of lung function.
    •

Rheumatology

  • Assessment and diagnosis of inflammatory arthritis.
  • Diagnosis and treatment for rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, lupus, ankylosing spondylitis.
  • Assessment of osteoarthritis.
  • Diagnosis, prevention and treatment of osteoarthritis.
  • Diagnosis of rare musculoskeletal disorders.
  • Our private physicians are leading experts in their chosen medical field and work closely together to offer expert diagnosis and care to private patients with complex conditions.
